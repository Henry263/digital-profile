Frequently Asked Questions

Answers about how the free QR profile app works, sharing, wallet passes, privacy, and more.

1. What is this app and how does it work?
This free app creates a sharable profile page from details you enter (name, photo, contact, and social links). The app generates a unique QR code, downloadable image, Apple Wallet pass, Google Wallet pass, and a shareable link. Scanning the QR or opening the link shows your profile instantly.
2. Is the app really free?
Yes ‚Äî core features (create profile, generate QR, download QR image, save to Apple/Google Wallet, share link) are free. Any premium/enterprise features (if offered later) will be announced separately.
3. Which platforms and devices are supported?
The QR/profile page is web-based and works on Android, iOS, and desktop browsers. Apple Wallet and Google Wallet passes are supported on devices that accept those passes. vCard (.vcf) downloads work across iOS and Android for quick contact saving.
4. Do recipients need to install an app to view profiles?
No. Recipients only need a QR scanner or a web browser. Scanning the QR opens the profile page instantly ‚Äî no app install required.
5. How do Apple Wallet and Google Wallet passes work?
After creating your profile you can save the QR as an Apple Wallet pass (.pkpass) or a Google Wallet pass. These behave like digital cards in the wallet app for quick access and offline presentation.
6. Can I edit my profile later?
Yes. Log into your account, update any field in the admin page, and save. Changes take effect immediately.
7. Is my data private and secure?
We use standard security practices to protect data. Sensitive cookies are HttpOnly, server endpoints require authentication, and tokens are stored securely. Review the privacy policy for full details and export/delete options.
8. How do I let people save my contact to their phone?
The profile page offers to save vCard (.vcf) download. On most phones this triggers the Contacts app to import the contact or allows one-tap saving.
9. What file types are available for download?
You can download the QR as PNG images, get an Apple Wallet pass, a Google Wallet pass, and a vCard (.vcf). Files are optimized for quality and sharing.
10. Can I share a profile link instead of a QR?
Yes ‚Äî every profile has a unique, short URL (the QR link page) you can copy and share anywhere: email, social bios, chat, or SMS.
